# Phases

- **Album Name:** Phases
- **Artist:** Chase Atlantic
- **Release Date:** June 28, 2019
- **Genre:** Alternative pop, dark R&B
- **Tracklist (5-10 tracks):**
  - Into It
  - Her
  - Love Is (Not) Easy
  - Paranoid
  - Stuckinmybrain
  - Heaven and Back
  - No Rainbows
- **Production Notes:** The album emphasizes nocturnal synth design, bass-heavy programming, and emotionally unstable storytelling. Chase Atlantic blends pop structure with darker textures, creating an immersive late-night atmosphere.
- **Chart Performance:** Built steady momentum through streaming and fan circulation, becoming one of the group’s most recognized full-length releases and a reliable entry point for new listeners.
- **Critical Reception:** Fans and critics alike viewed the album as a strong distillation of the band’s identity, praising its mood consistency and crossover appeal. Several tracks became staples of the group’s live set and online fan culture.
- **Awards:** While not a major awards-driven release, it significantly elevated the band’s profile and is often cited as a breakthrough album in retrospective discussions.

*Phases* captures Chase Atlantic at a moment when their sound became fully recognizable: moody, stylish, and emotionally reckless. Its cohesion helped turn a growing online following into long-term fan loyalty.
