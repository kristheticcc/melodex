# Keshi

- **Artist Name:** Keshi
- **Genre:** Alternative R&B, lo-fi pop, indie pop
- **Origin Country:** United States
- **Active Since:** 2017
- **Label:** Island Records
- **Biography:** Keshi is a singer-songwriter, producer, and multi-instrumentalist known for intimate vocals, understated beats, and emotionally direct songwriting. His music blends bedroom-pop sensitivity with polished alternative R&B production, creating a sound that feels both personal and widely accessible. Over time, he has grown from a streaming-driven breakout artist into a global touring act with a distinct visual and sonic identity.
- **Discography (list of albums with year):**
  - *Skeletons* (2022)
  - *GUTS* (2024)
- **Notable Achievements:** Built a dedicated international audience through streaming; praised for self-produced artistry; successfully translated a deeply personal sound into headline touring demand.
- **Fanbase Name:** Keshi Community

Keshi’s catalog emphasizes atmosphere and emotional restraint rather than vocal excess, which gives his songs unusual replay value. *GUTS* represents a more expansive chapter, broadening his sound without losing the closeness that defined his earlier releases.
