# TWICE

- **Artist Name:** TWICE
- **Genre:** K-pop, dance-pop, pop
- **Origin Country:** South Korea
- **Members (if group):** Nayeon, Jeongyeon, Momo, Sana, Jihyo, Mina, Dahyun, Chaeyoung, Tzuyu
- **Active Since:** 2015
- **Label:** JYP Entertainment
- **Biography:** TWICE rose from a survival-show origin into one of the defining girl groups of modern K-pop. Known first for bright, addictive singles and precision choreography, the group later expanded its sound into sleeker pop, disco, retro, and arena-ready performance music. Their longevity comes from a rare balance of mass appeal, distinct member chemistry, and a discography that has evolved without losing its core energy.
- **Discography (list of albums with year):**
  - *Twicetagram* (2017)
  - *Eyes Wide Open* (2020)
  - *Formula of Love: O+T=<3* (2021)
  - *Between 1&2* (2022)
  - *Ready To Be* (2023)
- **Notable Achievements:** Achieved major success in Korea and Japan; sold out large-scale international tours; became one of the highest-selling female K-pop acts of their era.
- **Fanbase Name:** ONCE

With *Ready To Be*, TWICE leaned further into stadium-scale pop while keeping the melodic charm that made their catalog enduringly popular. Their cross-market success made them a cornerstone act in any contemporary label archive.
