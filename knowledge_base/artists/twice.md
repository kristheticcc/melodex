# TWICE

- **Artist Name:** TWICE
- **Genre:** K-pop, dance-pop, pop
- **Origin Country:** South Korea
- **Members (if group):** Nayeon, Jeongyeon, Momo, Sana, Jihyo, Mina, Dahyun, Chaeyoung, Tzuyu
- **Active Since:** 2015
- **Label:** JYP Entertainment
- **Biography:** TWICE was formed through JYP Entertainment's survival show *Sixteen* and debuted in October 2015 with "Like OOH-AHH," quickly establishing a reputation for addictive hooks and clean, high-impact choreography. Their early run of hits, including "Cheer Up," "TT," and "Likey," made them one of the central acts in K-pop's late-2010s expansion and solidified their standing in both Korea and Japan. As the group matured, they broadened their sound into sleeker dance-pop, disco, retro-pop, and large-scale touring material while keeping their melodic immediacy intact. The group's longevity has also come from strong member chemistry, reliable live performance growth, and a willingness to explore unit, solo, and international work without weakening the core identity of TWICE itself. By the time of releases like *Ready To Be*, they had become one of the defining girl groups of their era and a benchmark for long-term K-pop career development.
- **Discography (list of albums with year):**
  - *Twicetagram* (2017) - Their first full album expanded TWICE's bright early formula into a fuller set of energetic pop songs led by the breakout single "Likey."
  - *Eyes Wide Open* (2020) - A stylish pivot toward mature synth-pop and retro textures that showed how comfortably the group could evolve its image.
  - *Formula of Love: O+T=<3* (2021) - A wide-ranging pop record that balanced playful songwriting with a more sophisticated, unit-friendly presentation.
  - *Between 1&2* (2022) - A compact release centered on confidence, chemistry, and polished b-sides that reinforced TWICE's seasoned-group appeal.
  - *Ready To Be* (2023) - A stage-ready mini album that sharpened the group's arena-pop instincts and supported a major global touring chapter.
- **Notable Achievements:** Achieved major success in Korea and Japan; sold out large-scale international tours; became one of the highest-selling female K-pop acts of their era.
- **Fanbase Name:** ONCE

With *Ready To Be*, TWICE leaned further into stadium-scale pop while keeping the melodic charm that made their catalog enduringly popular. Their cross-market success made them a cornerstone act in any contemporary label archive.

## Members

### Nayeon

- **Full name:** Im Na-yeon
- **Birth date and birthplace:** September 22, 1995; Seoul, South Korea
- **Role in group:** Lead vocalist, lead dancer
- **Personality and style notes:** Nayeon projects bright confidence and a playful kind of star power that has long made her one of TWICE's most recognizable centers. Her singing style is agile and energetic, and she often gives the group's livelier tracks their first spark of momentum.
- **Notable individual achievements or facts:** She became TWICE's first soloist with the successful release of *IM NAYEON*. Her voice is closely associated with many of the group's signature openings and hook lines.

### Jeongyeon

- **Full name:** Yoo Jeong-yeon
- **Birth date and birthplace:** November 1, 1996; Suwon, Gyeonggi, South Korea
- **Role in group:** Lead vocalist
- **Personality and style notes:** Jeongyeon is known for a grounded, straightforward presence that balances the group's brighter personalities. Vocally, she adds weight and steadiness, often giving TWICE songs a firmer emotional center.
- **Notable individual achievements or facts:** Her resilience through health-related breaks became a meaningful part of TWICE's later story and deepened fan appreciation for her presence. She is also often praised for the clarity and strength of her live vocals.

### Momo

- **Full name:** Hirai Momo
- **Birth date and birthplace:** November 9, 1996; Kyoto, Japan
- **Role in group:** Main dancer, sub vocalist, sub rapper
- **Personality and style notes:** Momo's performance style is sharp, athletic, and immediately identifiable, making her one of the defining dancers of her generation in K-pop. Off stage, her more relaxed and humorous personality creates a strong contrast with her intense choreographic precision.
- **Notable individual achievements or facts:** She is widely recognized for dance-focused stage performance and fancam popularity. Her work in the Japanese unit MISAMO further highlighted her versatility and cross-market appeal.

### Sana

- **Full name:** Minatozaki Sana
- **Birth date and birthplace:** December 29, 1996; Osaka, Japan
- **Role in group:** Sub vocalist
- **Personality and style notes:** Sana brings warmth, charm, and a naturally expressive delivery that has made her one of TWICE's most beloved personalities. Her performance style leans inviting rather than forceful, which helps sell both the group's cuter concepts and its more polished pop material.
- **Notable individual achievements or facts:** She became especially famous during TWICE's early breakout years for memorable killing parts and viral stage presence. Along with Mina and Momo, she is a key part of MISAMO.

### Jihyo

- **Full name:** Park Ji-hyo
- **Birth date and birthplace:** February 1, 1997; Guri, Gyeonggi, South Korea
- **Role in group:** Leader, main vocalist
- **Personality and style notes:** Jihyo combines visible drive with a highly dependable performance style, often functioning as TWICE's emotional and musical anchor. Her vocals are powerful without sounding disconnected from the group's pop core, which lets her elevate dramatic choruses while preserving accessibility.
- **Notable individual achievements or facts:** She debuted as TWICE's leader after a notably long trainee period, a background that contributed to her reputation for professionalism. Her solo project *ZONE* underlined her ability to carry a more performance-heavy sound on her own.

### Mina

- **Full name:** Myoui Mina
- **Birth date and birthplace:** March 24, 1997; San Antonio, Texas, United States
- **Role in group:** Main dancer, sub vocalist
- **Personality and style notes:** Mina is associated with elegance, restraint, and smooth movement, giving TWICE a different texture from the group's brighter and more extroverted members. Her voice often adds softness and composure, especially in songs that lean atmospheric or romantic.
- **Notable individual achievements or facts:** Her ballet background shaped her fluid stage style and became a defining part of her image. She also expanded her profile through MISAMO's Japanese activities.

### Dahyun

- **Full name:** Kim Da-hyun
- **Birth date and birthplace:** May 28, 1998; Seongnam, Gyeonggi, South Korea
- **Role in group:** Lead rapper, sub vocalist
- **Personality and style notes:** Dahyun is known for quick wit, strong variety instincts, and a lively presence that helps TWICE feel approachable beyond the stage. Musically, she brings crisp rhythmic delivery and a bright vocal tone that fits the group's upbeat catalog well.
- **Notable individual achievements or facts:** She has writing credits in TWICE's discography and became one of the group's most effective personalities in broadcast and fan-facing content. Her public image later expanded into acting work as well.

### Chaeyoung

- **Full name:** Son Chae-young
- **Birth date and birthplace:** April 23, 1999; Seoul, South Korea
- **Role in group:** Main rapper, sub vocalist
- **Personality and style notes:** Chaeyoung has long been the member most associated with artistic individuality inside TWICE, bringing a looser, more personal edge to the group's polished framework. Her rap and vocal parts often feel conversational and characterful, which adds texture to the group's smoother pop arrangements.
- **Notable individual achievements or facts:** She has participated in songwriting and is frequently recognized for her interest in visual art and fashion. Her individuality helped TWICE's more mature material feel less uniform and more self-defined.

### Tzuyu

- **Full name:** Chou Tzuyu
- **Birth date and birthplace:** June 14, 1999; Tainan, Taiwan
- **Role in group:** Lead dancer, sub vocalist, visual
- **Personality and style notes:** Tzuyu's image is built around poise and calmness, but her stage presence has steadily become more assured over the years. She often gives TWICE's performances a composed visual center, especially in songs that rely on sleek formations and clean silhouettes.
- **Notable individual achievements or facts:** As TWICE's Taiwanese member, she played a major role in the group's international visibility from the beginning. Her long-term growth as a performer is frequently cited by fans as one of the clearest examples of the group's development.

## Musical Style

TWICE's catalog is rooted in melodic pop built for immediate recall: strong choruses, rhythmic clarity, clean vocal arrangements, and choreography-friendly structure. Their early sound leaned bright and bubbly, but later releases pulled in synth-pop, disco, city-pop references, contemporary dance-pop, and more mature electronic textures without losing the sweetness that made them widely accessible. Lyrically, the group often moves between flirtation, confidence, longing, and self-assurance, with later work placing more emphasis on agency, glamour, and emotional steadiness than their earlier youthful material.
