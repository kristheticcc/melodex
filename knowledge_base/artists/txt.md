# TXT / Tomorrow X Together

- **Artist Name:** TXT / Tomorrow X Together
- **Genre:** K-pop, pop-rock, synth-pop
- **Origin Country:** South Korea
- **Members (if group):** Soobin, Yeonjun, Beomgyu, Taehyun, HueningKai
- **Active Since:** 2019
- **Label:** BIGHIT MUSIC
- **Biography:** TXT built a reputation for concept-driven pop that mixes youthful storytelling with ambitious genre shifts. Across bright debut material, moody rock-inflected singles, and emotionally charged albums, the group has shown unusual versatility while maintaining a strong narrative identity. Their work often explores growth, temptation, friendship, and anxiety, making their discography feel cohesive even when the production palette changes dramatically.
- **Discography (list of albums with year):**
  - *The Dream Chapter: STAR* (2019)
  - *The Chaos Chapter: FREEZE* (2021)
  - *minisode 2: Thursday's Child* (2022)
  - *The Name Chapter: TEMPTATION* (2023)
- **Notable Achievements:** Reached strong Billboard placements; became recognized for performance quality and album storytelling; expanded K-pop’s global reach among younger audiences.
- **Fanbase Name:** MOA

*The Name Chapter: TEMPTATION* marked a major commercial and artistic step forward, pairing glossy production with themes of escape and desire. It remains a key entry in TXT’s developing long-form album narrative.
