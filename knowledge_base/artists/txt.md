# TXT / Tomorrow X Together

- **Artist Name:** TXT / Tomorrow X Together
- **Genre:** K-pop, pop-rock, synth-pop
- **Origin Country:** South Korea
- **Members (if group):** Soobin, Yeonjun, Beomgyu, Taehyun, HueningKai
- **Active Since:** 2019
- **Label:** BIGHIT MUSIC
- **Biography:** TXT debuted in March 2019 under BIGHIT MUSIC with *The Dream Chapter: STAR*, arriving with a polished youth-pop concept that immediately drew attention both inside and outside Korea. Rather than staying in one sonic lane, the group quickly built a reputation for concept-driven albums that could shift from bright synth-pop to pop-rock, emo, alternative pop, and dance-oriented material while still feeling connected by shared narrative themes. Their catalog repeatedly returns to adolescence, identity, temptation, friendship, and escape, making each release feel like part of a larger coming-of-age story rather than a disconnected comeback cycle. Performance quality, strong visual worldbuilding, and consistent international charting helped turn them into one of the most globally visible fourth-generation boy groups. By the time *The Name Chapter: TEMPTATION* arrived, TXT had become especially known for making ambitious concept albums feel emotionally direct and commercially accessible at the same time.
- **Discography (list of albums with year):**
  - *The Dream Chapter: STAR* (2019) - A bright, emotionally open debut that introduced TXT's youth-focused storytelling and polished pop fundamentals.
  - *The Chaos Chapter: FREEZE* (2021) - A darker, more rock-leaning release that expanded the group's emotional range and global profile.
  - *minisode 2: Thursday's Child* (2022) - A compact breakup-themed project that translated adolescent confusion into sleek, high-drama pop.
  - *The Name Chapter: TEMPTATION* (2023) - A cohesive set about seduction, escapism, and collapse, wrapped in some of the group's most adventurous production to date.
- **Notable Achievements:** Reached strong Billboard placements; became recognized for performance quality and album storytelling; expanded K-pop’s global reach among younger audiences.
- **Fanbase Name:** MOA

*The Name Chapter: TEMPTATION* marked a major commercial and artistic step forward, pairing glossy production with themes of escape and desire. It remains a key entry in TXT’s developing long-form album narrative.

## Members

### Soobin

- **Full name:** Choi Soo-bin
- **Birth date and birthplace:** December 5, 2000; Sangnok-gu, Ansan, Gyeonggi, South Korea
- **Role in group:** Leader, vocalist, rapper
- **Personality and style notes:** Soobin has a gentle, dependable presence that makes him an effective emotional center for the group. His performance style is measured and clean, and he often helps balance TXT's more theatrical concepts with a sense of sincerity.
- **Notable individual achievements or facts:** He gained added visibility through MC work and variety appearances, which highlighted his calm humor and communication skills. Fans often point to his leadership style as one of the reasons TXT's group chemistry feels stable even during concept-heavy eras.

### Yeonjun

- **Full name:** Choi Yeon-jun
- **Birth date and birthplace:** September 13, 1999; Seoul, South Korea
- **Role in group:** Rapper, vocalist, dancer
- **Personality and style notes:** Yeonjun is often the member most associated with swagger, adaptability, and high-performance intensity. He moves easily between rap, vocals, and dance-centered parts, which gives TXT a more elastic edge when songs lean darker or more fashion-forward.
- **Notable individual achievements or facts:** He was well known even before debut for his trainee reputation and later became a frequent standout in performance discussions. His fashion visibility and stage confidence also made him one of TXT's most individually recognizable members.

### Beomgyu

- **Full name:** Choi Beom-gyu
- **Birth date and birthplace:** March 13, 2001; Daegu, South Korea
- **Role in group:** Vocalist, dancer
- **Personality and style notes:** Beomgyu brings a mix of playfulness and sensitivity that fits TXT's coming-of-age storytelling particularly well. His tone and on-stage expression often add a slightly wistful color, especially when the group moves into rock or emotionally turbulent material.
- **Notable individual achievements or facts:** He is closely associated with TXT's band-inflected image in live and concept content because of his comfort with guitar-centered presentation. Fans also frequently highlight his ability to toggle between variety energy and more introspective performances.

### Taehyun

- **Full name:** Kang Tae-hyun
- **Birth date and birthplace:** February 5, 2002; Gangnam-gu, Seoul, South Korea
- **Role in group:** Vocalist
- **Personality and style notes:** Taehyun is known for a focused, articulate personality and a vocal style that brings precision to TXT's more demanding songs. He often comes across as analytical in interviews, and that sense of intent carries into performances that require emotional clarity rather than just scale.
- **Notable individual achievements or facts:** His stable live singing has made him a key pillar in TXT's performance reputation. He is also recognized for helping communicate the group's concepts clearly in interviews and behind-the-scenes content.

### HueningKai

- **Full name:** Kai Kamal Huening
- **Birth date and birthplace:** August 14, 2002; Honolulu, Hawaii, United States
- **Role in group:** Vocalist, dancer
- **Personality and style notes:** HueningKai adds brightness and openness to TXT's lineup, both in personality and in vocal color. Even when the group's music turns darker, he often supplies a sense of lift that keeps the songs emotionally dynamic rather than one-note.
- **Notable individual achievements or facts:** His multicultural background gave him a natural role in TXT's international visibility from early on. He has also earned praise for songwriting contributions and for how easily he moves between playful and dramatic concepts.

## Musical Style

TXT's sound is built around movement between genres rather than strict loyalty to one of them. Across their discography, they combine synth-pop, pop-rock, emo-pop, trap accents, bright dance tracks, and cinematic ballad textures, usually tied together by polished vocal layering and an album-first sense of sequencing. Their lyrics frequently revolve around adolescence, desire, friendship, panic, fantasy, and emotional transformation, giving even their stylistic pivots the feeling of chapters in one long narrative.
