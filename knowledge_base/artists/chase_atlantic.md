# Chase Atlantic

- **Artist Name:** Chase Atlantic
- **Genre:** Alternative pop, dark R&B, pop-rock
- **Origin Country:** Australia
- **Members (if group):** Mitchel Cave, Christian Anthony, Clinton Cave
- **Active Since:** 2014
- **Label:** Fearless Records
- **Biography:** Chase Atlantic was formed in Australia by brothers Mitchel and Clinton Cave with longtime collaborator Christian Anthony, evolving out of earlier projects before settling into a darker, more stylized identity. The trio built early momentum online through a mix of moody singles, self-directed visual branding, and a sound that fused alternative pop, trap textures, guitar work, and R&B phrasing. As their streaming audience grew, they developed a reputation for sleek, nocturnal production and emotionally messy songwriting that felt closer to internet-era alt culture than to conventional band marketing. Albums like *Chase Atlantic* and *Phases* sharpened that identity into something highly recognizable, helping the group expand from digital cult status into a durable international touring act. Their longevity comes from how tightly their aesthetic, production choices, and lyrical world all reinforce each other.
- **Discography (list of albums with year):**
  - *Chase Atlantic* (2017) - A breakout full-length that organized the trio's alt-pop, R&B, and rock instincts into a more complete statement of identity.
  - *Phases* (2019) - A late-night, emotionally volatile record that many listeners see as the clearest early definition of the Chase Atlantic sound.
  - *BEAUTY IN DEATH* (2021) - A darker and more expansive project that pushed their glossy nihilism into bigger, more cinematic territory.
- **Notable Achievements:** Cultivated a passionate global fanbase; sustained long-term streaming performance; became a recognizable crossover act between pop, rock, and alternative scenes.
- **Fanbase Name:** C4

*Phases* is often seen as the project that sharpened the group’s identity, emphasizing nocturnal production and emotionally volatile songwriting. It remains one of the most representative releases in the Chase Atlantic catalog.

## Members

### Mitchel Cave

- **Full name:** Mitchel Cave
- **Birth date and birthplace:** December 2, 1996; Cairns, Queensland, Australia
- **Role in group:** Lead vocalist, programmer, primary songwriter
- **Personality and style notes:** Mitchel is the emotional and stylistic focal point of Chase Atlantic, bringing a moody vocal delivery that can shift from breathy restraint to full pop release. His writing and performance style tend to center atmosphere first, which is a major reason the band's songs feel so visually and emotionally unified.
- **Notable individual achievements or facts:** He is widely regarded as one of the chief architects of the band's production identity. Before Chase Atlantic fully took off, he also appeared on *The X Factor Australia* in an earlier group project.

### Christian Anthony

- **Full name:** Christian Anthony
- **Birth date and birthplace:** September 23, 1997; Cairns, Queensland, Australia
- **Role in group:** Vocalist, rhythm guitarist, songwriter
- **Personality and style notes:** Christian often supplies a rougher edge to the trio's sound, both vocally and in the band's more guitar-forward material. His stage presence feels less polished in a conventional pop sense and more instinctive, which suits the group's messy, late-night emotional world.
- **Notable individual achievements or facts:** He has been central to the group's live identity because of how naturally he bridges the rock and pop sides of their catalog. His phrasing frequently gives Chase Atlantic songs some of their most immediate bite.

### Clinton Cave

- **Full name:** Clinton Cave
- **Birth date and birthplace:** July 4, 1993; Cairns, Queensland, Australia
- **Role in group:** Lead guitarist, saxophonist, backing vocalist, producer
- **Personality and style notes:** Clinton's role is often felt through texture as much as visibility, since he helps shape the band's darker sonic details and instrumental character. His style brings a cool, technical layer to the group, especially when guitar lines or saxophone touches are used to thicken the atmosphere.
- **Notable individual achievements or facts:** He had an online following before the band fully formed through music content and collaborations. Within Chase Atlantic, he is a major part of the production and arrangement backbone that separates the group from more generic alt-pop acts.

## Musical Style

Chase Atlantic's music sits at the intersection of alternative pop, dark R&B, trap programming, and pop-rock, usually wrapped in glossy, after-hours production. Their songs favor deep low end, hazy synth layers, processed vocal textures, and hooks that feel melodic enough for pop while still carrying a restless, slightly intoxicated mood. Lyrically, the band returns again and again to obsession, self-destruction, nightlife, sex, intoxication, emotional spirals, and the blurred line between confidence and collapse.
