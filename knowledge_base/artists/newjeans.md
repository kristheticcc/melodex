# NewJeans

- **Artist Name:** NewJeans
- **Genre:** K-pop, pop, R&B
- **Origin Country:** South Korea
- **Members (if group):** Minji, Hanni, Danielle, Haerin, Hyein
- **Active Since:** 2022
- **Label:** ADOR
- **Biography:** NewJeans emerged in July 2022 with an unconventional rollout built around surprise music video releases for "Attention" and "Hype Boy," immediately separating the group from standard K-pop debut campaigns. Their early music fused Y2K-inspired pop, soft R&B textures, and understated hooks, while their visual identity emphasized casual styling and everyday movement rather than maximal spectacle. The group's first year was defined by dominant digital chart performance in South Korea, rapid international streaming growth, and unusually strong crossover attention for such a new act. Releases like *OMG* and *Get Up* turned that momentum into staying power, proving the group could translate minimalist production into both commercial scale and cultural influence. Even in a crowded generation of girl groups, NewJeans became a reference point for how contemporary K-pop could feel lighter, more conversational, and more globally fluid.
- **Discography (list of albums with year):**
  - *New Jeans* (2022) - A brisk debut project that introduced the group's relaxed vocal delivery, Y2K styling, and instantly recognizable melodic ease.
  - *OMG* (2023) - A focused release built around "Ditto" and "OMG," sharpening their soft-groove sound and confirming their breakout was sustainable.
  - *Get Up* (2023) - A tighter, more global-facing set that condensed their identity into sleek, highly replayable songs with polished pop precision.
- **Notable Achievements:** Broke multiple Korean streaming records early in their career; earned strong global chart visibility; widely praised for reshaping the visual and sonic direction of fourth-generation K-pop.
- **Fanbase Name:** Bunnies

NewJeans built momentum through songs that sounded effortless while still being meticulously produced. Their releases, including *OMG*, helped establish the group as both a domestic powerhouse and an international crossover act with unusually broad appeal.

## Members

### Minji

- **Full name:** Kim Min-ji
- **Birth date and birthplace:** May 7, 2004; Chuncheon, Gangwon, South Korea
- **Role in group:** Vocalist, dancer
- **Personality and style notes:** Minji is often read as the group's grounding presence, with a calm screen demeanor and an understated kind of charisma that suits NewJeans' natural image. Her performance style leans clean and controlled rather than flashy, which helps anchor the group's softer, more conversational songs.
- **Notable individual achievements or facts:** She was the first member publicly introduced through the "Attention" rollout and quickly became a recognizable face in fashion and beauty campaigns. Fans often point to her composure in live stages and interviews as a big part of the group's polished first impression.

### Hanni

- **Full name:** Hanni Pham
- **Birth date and birthplace:** October 6, 2004; Melbourne, Victoria, Australia
- **Role in group:** Vocalist, dancer

## Musical Style

NewJeans built their identity around minimal but highly intentional pop production: crisp drum programming, soft R&B chords, uncluttered vocal stacks, and hooks that feel conversational instead of oversized. Their songs often draw on late-1990s and 2000s references without turning into strict nostalgia exercises, blending UK garage, Jersey club accents, airy synth-pop, and smooth mid-tempo grooves into something immediately contemporary. Lyrically, their catalog tends to focus on youthful attraction, uncertainty, emotional drift, and everyday intimacy, with a tone that sounds casual on the surface but is usually very controlled in execution.
