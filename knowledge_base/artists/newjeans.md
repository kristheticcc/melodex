# NewJeans

- **Artist Name:** NewJeans
- **Genre:** K-pop, pop, R&B
- **Origin Country:** South Korea
- **Members (if group):** Minji, Hanni, Danielle, Haerin, Hyein
- **Active Since:** 2022
- **Label:** ADOR
- **Biography:** NewJeans emerged with a refreshingly understated sound that blended Y2K-inspired pop, soft R&B textures, and highly approachable hooks. The group quickly became known for natural styling, strong digital performance, and a youthful concept that felt contemporary without leaning too heavily on trend-chasing. Their catalog balances breezy singles with polished production, helping them stand out as one of the most influential girl groups of their generation.
- **Discography (list of albums with year):**
  - *New Jeans* (2022)
  - *OMG* (2023)
  - *Get Up* (2023)
- **Notable Achievements:** Broke multiple Korean streaming records early in their career; earned strong global chart visibility; widely praised for reshaping the visual and sonic direction of fourth-generation K-pop.
- **Fanbase Name:** Bunnies

NewJeans built momentum through songs that sounded effortless while still being meticulously produced. Their releases, including *OMG*, helped establish the group as both a domestic powerhouse and an international crossover act with unusually broad appeal.
